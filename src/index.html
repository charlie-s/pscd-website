Professional Draftsman Services

My name is Paul Sansone and I am the owner of Sansone Contracting and Design. I’ve been a professional draftsman for over 35 years, with experience in not only creating structural drawings for residential construction projects, but building them as well. Firsthand experience as a contractor has shown me value and need for custom home design.

In today's design/build world, your specific needs can be explored in a creative, technically sound and economical way, many times without signing on with a large architecture firm where you may end up paying for more than you had in mind. So if you have an idea of what you want - be it a room addition, a three-car garage, or 3,000-square foot house - fill out the contact form below and let’s make your ideas come to life.
